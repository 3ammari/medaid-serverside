Dawa'a is an open source project aims to assist people in Sudan finding their medication ,as this is an issue addressed by almost everyone in the country where finding a specific medication -possibly be a life saving medication- might be a tough task.

Project is fairly simple and the idea was discussed with many people ,some are end-users some are pharmacists all supporting the idea of the project

project consists of two major parts :

  • Server-side.
  • client.
